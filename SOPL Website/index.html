Committed to quality

Quality is not a compromise. From duffles to slings, and everything in between, no details are overlooked. Revisiting existing methods to improve processes and maximize efficiency is an everyday goal. Through quality control procedures, defectives are altered and unsatisfactory pieces are rejected. We have an extensive system in place across various stages to be certain that we only deliver the best.

Our process

Leathers are sourced from local tanneries on a regular basis. All raw materials are sourced from a trusted network of suppliers - local and foreign. Our in-house machinery is acquired from Adler, OMAC and Juki. From sourcing to product completion, goods are inspected consistently to ensure quality standards and expectations are being met.

Crafted with care

We respect and celebrate every little detail. Every addition to an item has a purpose - be it trendy and/or utility. Along with our timeless classics, new designs are always being explored and developed through research and constructive brainstorming with our heads of departments and the in-house design team. We love what we do and we know you will too.
